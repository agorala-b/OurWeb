Thu Trang Nguyen was born in Hanoi, Vietnam, in 2004. She is currently pursuing a dual Bachelor’s degree in Marketing at the International School, Vietnam National University, Hanoi, Vietnam and Bachelor of Business (Marketing) from HELP University, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Her research activities include Brand Equity, Consumer Behavior, Brand Management, the impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) on business enterprises, Social Sciences, ESG and Large language model (LLM).
